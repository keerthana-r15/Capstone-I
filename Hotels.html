    Brief description


    With comfortable, modern studios and guest rooms set amidst

    spectacular mountain scenery,

    Sterling Manali offers everything for a relaxing and memorable escape.

    Studios and en-suite guest rooms include TVs, personal safes and tea- and

    coffee-making facilities. Apartments feature kitchens and balconies.

    Sterling Manali offers a variety of amenities, including an Ayurvedic wellness spa

    and fully staffed Travel desk where guests can hire vehicles, book tours and

    make air and train travel reservations.

    Numerous planned activities such as nature hikes and sightseeing are offered,

    and the hotel offers complimentary on-site parking.

    The hotel’s in-house restaurant features local and French cuisine and serves breakfast

    and snacks throughout the day. Room service is available 24 hours.

    Sterling White Mist offers sightseeing tour packages to Hadimba Devi temple,

    Tibetan monastery and Vashisht sulphur hot springs.

    Solang Valley, popular for adventure sports, is just over five miles from the hotel.

    Brief description


    Snow Valley Resorts is an enchanting hideaway surrounded by breathtaking

    snow capped Himalayan Mountains and lush green pinewood forests in the

    picturesque town of Manali, in India’s Himachal Pradesh province.

    The resort is the largest in the charming Log Hut area which is just

    02 Kilometres from Manali Bus Stand and is surrounded by vast expanse

    of the apple orchards and offers wonderfully fresh, clean air.

    Relax in this elegant luxury mountainside hotel offering 52 rooms

    and suites that bask in the daytime sunshine creating a warm and welcoming retreat.

    The tasteful accommodation has local design touches with rich colours, wooden floors

    and plush furnishings along with modern amenities and optional stunning views.

    The resort facilities allow guests to dine in style overlooking fabulous landscapes

    at the Valley View Restaurant, The Orchard and The Terrace.

    Fun activities take place in the games room and the conference hall

    can host large events and meetings. Outside the resort there is a whole range of sports

    and adventure activities to join from River Rafting and Skiing to Gondola Rides.
